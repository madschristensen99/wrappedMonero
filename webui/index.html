How It Works

The WXMR Bridge enables atomic swaps between Monero (XMR) and Wrapped Monero (WXMR) using time-locked addresses.

XMR to WXMR:

  1. You send XMR to a time-locked Monero address.
  2. The bridge oracle verifies your transaction on the Monero blockchain.
  3. WXMR tokens are minted and sent to your EVM address.

WXMR to XMR:

  1. You lock WXMR tokens in the bridge contract.
  2. The bridge oracle detects this and sends XMR to your Monero address.
  3. The locked WXMR tokens are burned.

All swaps are protected by time-locks. If a swap fails to complete, you can reclaim your funds after the timelock expires (24 hours by default).

Liquidity Provider Info

As a liquidity provider, you commit XMR to the bridge protocol which enables XMR-WXMR swaps for users.

Benefits:

  • Earn 0.27% fees from all swaps processed by the bridge
  • Fees are distributed proportionally based on your share of the liquidity pool

Requirements:

  • Minimum liquidity contribution: 0.1 XMR
  • Maximum 1 active liquidity pool per address
  • 24-hour cooldown period between withdrawals

When you provide liquidity, you receive an equivalent amount of WXMR tokens which represent your position. When you withdraw liquidity, these WXMR tokens are burned.
